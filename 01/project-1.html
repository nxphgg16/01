Designing the E-Menu for “YEN SUSHI & SAKE PUB”

During this two-week project with YEN SUSHI & SAKE PUB, I worked as the product designer during the all phase. Upon implementation I was head of UI, focusing on visual and interaction design.

Project overview:

YEN Sushi & Sake Pub is one of the first Japanese Restaurant chains in Saigon, wanting to enhance its dining experience by introducing digital menus to better serve customers. This case study details the user experience (UX) design process for creating user-friendly and effective digital menus.

Project goals:

Improve the dining experience for customers by making menu navigation and item selection more user-friendly. Increase efficiency for both customers and restaurant staff by reducing wait times and enhancing order accuracy. Maintain a consistent and visually appealing brand image throughout the digital menu

iteration:

outcome:

The launch of the new digital menu for YEN SUSHI & SAKE PUB resulted in several notable improvements:

User-Friendly Navigation: Customers found it easier to browse the menu and select items, reducing wait times and improving overall satisfaction.

Efficiency: With the integration of the e-menu into the POS system, order accuracy and speed improved, leading to shorter wait times for customers and increased table turnover.

Brand Consistency: The digital menu maintained the restaurant's branding while offering a modern and visually appealing interface

Accessibility: The inclusion of dietary and allergen information catered to a wider range of customer needs.

Positive Feedback: Customer feedback was overwhelmingly positive, with many praising the e-menu's ease of use and improved dining experience.

lessons learned:

User-Centered Design:The first and foremost lesson is to center the design around the needs and preferences of the users

Clear and Accessible InformationIn a digital menu, clarity is key. Ensure that menu items, descriptions, and prices are presented in a clear, readable font. Accessibility is vital for all customers

Intuitive Navigation:User-friendly navigation is critical. The menu should be organized logically, with clear categories and filters for easy browsing. The user should find what they want quickly and efficiently

Collaboration with Developers:Effective communication and a detailed handoff process are essential for a smooth development phase.
