Designing the QR code Menu for “FUTA - Phương Trang”

Project overview:

With the goal of serving customers to rest at the rest stop. FUTA wants to build a QR code ordering system to save customers time and control orders accurately.

Project goals:

Save time and manage orders accurately

Enhance the shopping experience with user-friendly navigation for customers traveling or buying gifts back home

Maintain a consistent and attractive brand image

My roles: Visual Design, Prototype

Duration: 12 Dec 2022 - 15 Jan 2023

Tool: Figma

stucture:

ui pattern:

Visual Design:

Maintains FUTA's existing brand colors while enhancing overall visual appeal. Make sure that the images are high quality.

Lessons Learned:

Consistent design elements:Maintain consistency in design elements throughout the menu. Consistency in design provides a unified and aesthetic experience, making it easier for users to navigate and understand menus.

Consider mobile-friendliness:Since QR code menus are often accessed on mobile devices, optimize the visual design for smaller screens. Make sure the menu adapts to a variety of device sizes and orientations.

Visual cues for interaction:Clear and intuitive interaction cues eliminate ambiguity and facilitate users' ability to perform actions effectively.
